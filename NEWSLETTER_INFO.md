## Where to Find Subscribers

New subscribers will appear in **two places** depending on whether they've confirmed their email:

### 1. Unconfirmed Subscribers
**Link:** https://app.convertkit.com/subscribers/unconfirmed

New signups appear here FIRST. They stay here until they click the confirmation link in their email.

### 2. Confirmed Subscribers
**Link:** https://app.convertkit.com/subscribers

After clicking the confirmation email, subscribers move here and can receive your newsletters.
